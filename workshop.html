The story

This project was for a coffee shop due to be launched in autumn 2018. Its mission is to serve locals with freshly brewed coffee and snacks, in a friendly and cosy space.

I designed the brand identity, including the logo and its applications. The main challenge was to ensure that everything matched up with the owners’ vision. From this I learnt the importance of communication with and understanding of the client.
